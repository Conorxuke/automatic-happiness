If all green: a verbal “I'm in” + agreement to one concrete first step.

⚑ Watch-outs — hold these in your head

Capacity is the real risk

He's a founder mid-scale (NDRC / EI / PorterShed-backed, four markets, trade show in two weeks). 2–3 days/wk is a big pull. Test it openly today, don't assume.

Scope vs budget gap

Leadership approved 3 agents, €15–20k. The blueprint is now 5 agents / 8 weeks. Decide which you're committing to — and whether the envelope still holds.

Founder, not contractor

Pitch the case-study / credential upside, not “come do bespoke consulting.” The honest offer is a clean, finite, well-paid build — not an open tap on his time.

Listen for: genuine interest in the problem · a feasible time commitment · curiosity about the bigger picture. Best outcome: “Here's exactly how I'd build it — and yes, I'm in.”
